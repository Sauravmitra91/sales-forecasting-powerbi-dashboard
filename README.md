# 📊 Sales Forecasting & Demand Analysis Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify trends, patterns, and business insights. The goal is to support better decision-making and improve demand planning.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Excel / CSV  

---

## 📈 Key Features
- KPI Metrics (Total Sales, Profit, Quantity)  
- Sales Trend Analysis  
- Category & Product Performance  
- Growth Analysis (MoM %)  
- Interactive Filters  

---

## 📊 Insights
- Identified top-performing product categories  
- Observed sales fluctuations over time  
- Highlighted demand patterns and growth trends  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Optimize inventory during peak periods  
- Focus on high-performing categories  
- Use data-driven strategies for demand planning
